BEFORE YOU REACH OUT

Common Questions About Working With Us

How do we get started?

Start with a free consultation. Share your scope, site, and timeline, and we will recommend the right service path before any design work begins.

What does a consultation cost?

The initial consultation is free and carries no obligation. It is a planning conversation to map scope, not a sales pitch.

When is the best time to contact you?

Before plans, budgets, and permits are locked in. Early alignment is where we protect the most options for your scope and cost.

Do you work across the Bay Area?

Yes. We are based in San Jose and support homeowners on remodels, additions, ADUs, and custom homes throughout the Bay Area.
