QUICK FAQ

Common First Questions

What is the best stage to contact UniqHaus?

Before plans and permits are finalized. Early alignment improves scope control, budget accuracy, and schedule reliability.

Do you handle permitting and agency coordination?

Yes. Permit documentation and coordination are integrated into the core process rather than outsourced after design.

Can you support both remodel and ground-up projects?

Yes. UniqHaus supports remodels, ADUs and additions, custom homes, and selected commercial environments when the scope fits our studio model.
