HOW WE WORK

A Process Built for
Clarity and Continuity

The process is designed to answer three questions early: what are we building, what does it require, and what must be decided before construction begins.

01

Initial Planning Conversation

We begin with a focused conversation to understand your goals, constraints, scope, and timeline before any design decisions are made.

02

Project Setup and Alignment

Your project is organized into one working structure for communication, milestones, decisions, and scope review.

03

Concept and Spatial Planning

We develop the foundational spatial framework — documenting existing conditions and exploring layout, flow, and program.

04

3D Visualization and Design Review

Your project is reviewed in full detail before construction begins, helping align layout, materials, and major decisions earlier.

05

Permitting and Agency Coordination

UniqHaus manages the full permitting process, coordinating with local agencies to ensure full approval before construction begins.

06

Cost Validation and Scope Confirmation

With developed plans, we prepare line-item cost expectations and confirm scope before field work begins.

07

Construction and Ongoing Coordination

Construction moves forward with continued design oversight so execution stays aligned with drawings, selections, and approved scope.

08

Completion and Project Closeout

A thorough final walkthrough, full documentation, and a seamless transition into your finished space.
